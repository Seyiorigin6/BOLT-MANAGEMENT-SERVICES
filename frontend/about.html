About Bolt

Connecting people safely and affordably; one ride at a time.

Who we are

Bolt Management Services is a student-led ridesharing concept founded in November 2025 with a vision to transform everyday transportation. Created to provide a safer, faster, and more convenient way for people to move around their cities, our platform focuses on simplicity, trust, and reliability for all types of riders.

We are committed to making movement easier for everyone. Our service is built on core principles of safety, affordability, and convenience, ensuring that every ride is reliable from start to finish. All drivers on our platform are verified, pricing is transparent, and trip activity can be tracked from pick-up to drop-off for added peace of mind.

Beyond being a transport solution, Bolt Management Services represents fresh innovation and forward-thinking design. Our goal is to leverage technology to improve urban mobility, reduce everyday stress, and make commuting accessible and seamless for everyone. Whether you’re heading to work, running errands, or exploring the city, we aim to make your ride smoother, safer, and more enjoyable.

Our Mission

To make campus travel safe, affordable and stress-free while creating earning opportunities for drivers.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and convenient local transport partner for university communities.
