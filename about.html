About Bolt

Connecting people safely and affordably; one ride at a time.

Who we are

Bolt Management Services is a student-led ridesharing concept built specifically for campus communities. Founded in November 2025, our platform was created to solve a simple but important problem: providing fast, safe, and dependable transportation for students, staff, and visitors within and around the university environment.

We are committed to making movement easier for everyone on campus. Our service is built on core principles of safety, affordability, and convenience, ensuring that every ride is reliable from start to finish. All drivers on our platform are verified, pricing is transparent, and trip activity can be tracked from pick-up to drop-off for added peace of mind.

More than just a transport solution, Bolt Management Services represents innovation by students, for students. We are passionate about using technology to enhance mobility, improve campus experience, and foster a more connected community. Our goal is to make everyday commuting simple, stress-free, and accessible to all.

Our Mission

To make campus travel safe, affordable and stress-free while creating earning opportunities for drivers.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and convenient local transport partner for university communities.
